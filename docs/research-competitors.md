# Konkur Arshad Preparation - Market Research

## Overview
Research on best platforms, tools, and apps for Konkur Arshad (Iranian Master's Entrance Exam) preparation.

---

## Top Iranian Platforms

### 1. Modaresane Sharif (مدرسان شریف)
- **URL:** https://www.modaresanesharif.ac.ir/
- **Reputation:** Highly recommended by students, especially for Master's level
- **Features:**
  - Official license from Ministry of Science
  - Comprehensive prep courses
  - Quality textbooks and materials
  - Mock exams with high statistical population
- **Student Feedback:** "Best for comprehensive prep, high-quality materials"

### 2. Mahan Institute (موسسه ماهان)
- **URL:** https://www.mahan.ac.ir/
- **Features:**
  - Both Master's and PhD prep
  - Online and in-person classes
  - Covers: Management, Engineering, Psychology, Law, Accounting, etc.
  - Book packages for each field
  - Free consultation
  - "Mahan Yar" study planning system
  - Installment payment options
- **Strengths:** Wide field coverage, modern platform

### 3. Parseh Institute (موسسه پارسه)
- **URL:** https://myparseh.com/
- **Experience:** 28 years in the field
- **Features:**
  - Top instructors
  - Data Structures & Algorithms courses praised
  - Good language courses
- **Student Feedback:** "Good for IT/CS fields, quality varies by subject"

## Student Recommendations Summary

Based on forum discussions (Manesht, Hupaa, Tarafdari, Iran-Eng):

| Aspect | Best Option | Notes |
|--------|-------------|-------|
| Overall Quality | Modaresane Sharif | Highest student satisfaction |
| Mock Exams | Modaresane Sharif | Largest test-taker population |
| Engineering | Modaresane Sharif / Parseh | Varies by specific field |
| Management | Mahan / Modaresane Sharif | Strong competition |
| Books Only | Mix sources | "Use best book per subject, not single publisher" |

**Key Insight:** Students recommend mixing resources - use best teacher/book per subject rather than committing to one institute.

## Key Features Students Want

Based on research:

1. **Past Exam Questions** - Categorized by year, subject, topic
2. **Detailed Answers** - Not just keys, but explanations
3. **Progress Tracking** - See improvement over time
4. **Smart Scheduling** - AI-based study plans
5. **Mock Exams** - Simulated test environment
6. **Mobile Access** - Study anywhere
7. **Subject-wise Organization** - Easy navigation
8. **Spaced Repetition** - For memorization-heavy subjects

---

## MDP Competitive Advantages (Opportunities)

Based on gaps in current market:

1. **Free Access to Past Exams** - Most charge for this
2. **Better Categorization** - By topic within subjects
3. **Modern UI/UX** - Many Iranian sites have dated interfaces
4. **Spaced Repetition Integration** - Not common in Iranian platforms
5. **Community Features** - Q&A, discussions
6. **AI-Powered Analysis** - Identify weak areas
